Contract of Services – Engr. Alvarado

CONTRACT OF SERVICES

This Contract of Services ("Contract") is entered into this 5th day of May 2026 by and between BICOL UNIVERSITY, a state university duly organized and existing under Philippine laws, with principal office at Legazpi City, Albay, represented herein by its President, PROF. FERDINAND PASIA, Ed.D., hereinafter referred to as the "University";

— AND —

ENGR. RAFAEL M. ALVARADO, of legal age, Filipino, and a resident of Daraga, Albay, hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider".

WITNESSETH:

WHEREAS, the University is in need of technical consultancy services for the upgrading of its electrical systems in the College of Engineering building;

WHEREAS, the Service Provider has the qualifications, skills, and expertise necessary for the aforesaid services;

NOW THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the foregoing premises and the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties hereto agree as follows:

Section 1. Scope of Work. The Service Provider agrees to render technical consultancy services for the electrical upgrade project of the College of Engineering, including assessment, design, and supervision of works, for the period from May 10, 2026 to August 10, 2026.

Section 2. Compensation. The University agrees to pay the Service Provider the total amount of ONE HUNDRED TWENTY THOUSAND PESOS (PHP 120,000.00), payable in three (3) equal installments upon completion of each phase.

Section 3. Obligations of the Service Provider. The Service Provider shall: (a) perform the services with utmost diligence and professional competence; (b) comply with all applicable laws and regulations; (c) submit progress reports every two weeks.
